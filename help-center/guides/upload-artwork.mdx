## Overview

Upload your own art to participate in games or share with others.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In">
    ````
    Go to artistgame.com and log into your account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to 'My Artworks'">
    ````
    Click your profile and select 'My Artworks' from the menu.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Upload'">
    ````
    Choose an image file (PNG or JPG).
    Add a title and description.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    ````
    Press the 'Upload' button to finish your submission.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
