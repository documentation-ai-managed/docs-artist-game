## Account Creation

To get started with Artist Game, you'll need an account.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://artistgame.com/signup`
2. Fill in your email and choose a password
3. Click "Sign Up"
4. Check your email for a confirmation link
5. Click the link to verify your account

## Notes

Your account lets you join games, upload artwork, and save progress.
